Pesto Pasta

Description

This versatile pesto pasta recipe is easy to customize by adding your favorite protein, if you like. Chicken, sausage, and chickpeas are all delicious mix-ins.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh basil, washed
  • 1/2 bunch fresh parsley, washed
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup walnut halves
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 can NESTLÉ® Media Crema
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 package spaghetti (200 g)

Steps

  1. For the pesto, blend basil leaves with parsley, chicken bouillon, garlic powder, olive oil, and walnuts. Add NESTLÉ® Media Crema and mix well. Heat
  2. In a pan, heat butter with pre-cooked and drained spaghetti; add the blended mixture and cook for 5 minutes over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Serve
  3. Serve, garnish with walnuts and basil leaves. Enjoy.
